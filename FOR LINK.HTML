Christopher Nkunku

French footballer

Christopher Alan Nkunku is a French professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder, second striker or forward for Premier League club Chelsea and the France national team. Nkunku is a graduate of the Paris Saint-Germain Academy and made his professional debut for the club in December 2015.
