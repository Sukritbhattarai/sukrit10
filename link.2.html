Mykhailo Mudryk

Ukrainian footballer

Mykhailo Petrovych Mudryk, also known as "Misha", is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a left winger for Premier League club Chelsea and the Ukraine national team. Mudryk began his career in the academies of Metalist Kharkiv and Dnipro, before moving to Shakhtar Donetsk in 2016.
